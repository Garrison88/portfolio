A Bit About Myself

I love solving problems and writing efficient code, and I believe consolidating code can be as productive as writing it.

My obsession with programming began with a deep dive into Android. I designed, developed, and deployed a logistics app for a Toronto-based junk removal company. More recently, I completed a contract to build a fish tracking app for a First Nations community in Northern B.C.

Along with native development, I also use Dart and the Flutter framework to build for both Android and iOS with one codebase.
